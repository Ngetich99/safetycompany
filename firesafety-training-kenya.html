fire safety training

At Hazard Control Technologies Ltd, we offer comprehensive Fire Safety Training designed to equip employees with the knowledge and practical skills needed to respond effectively in the event of a fire. Our training covers fire prevention, safe evacuation procedures, proper use of fire extinguishers, and understanding different classes of fire. Delivered by certified professionals, the program meets regulatory standards and is tailored to your workplace environment. This training not only helps you comply with Kenyan fire safety regulations but also builds confidence among staff to act quickly and safely during emergencies.
