Who We Are

Hazard Control Technologies Ltd is a licensed safety consultancy firm dedicated to advancing workplace safety standards in Kenya. We specialize in Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) audits, Fire Safety training, First Aid, and the supply of certified fire protection equipment. We are officially registered and certified by the Government of Kenya, through the Directorate of Occupational Safety and Health Services (DOSH) and the National Industrial Training Authority (NITA), to carry out training and audits nationwide. Our team is made up of passionate, experienced professionals committed to equipping individuals and organizations with the skills and tools needed to prevent accidents, respond to emergencies, and stay compliant with legal safety requirements. At our core, we believe that safety is not just a legal obligation—it’s a life-saving investment.
